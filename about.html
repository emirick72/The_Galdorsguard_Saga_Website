ABOUT THE SERIES

Torren Sylvani, a half-elf bounty hunter, only cares about two things: himself and getting paid. When a bounty mission goes wrong, Torren chooses to betray his boss and begins guarding both the wanted Prince Albern and ex-High Mage Chiron as they go on the run from the Satheran Monarchy. While on the run, the trio meet many new allies and old foes, which eventually culminates in them banding together to stop an ancient necromantic cult.

The Galdorsguard Saga originally started in 2020 as a planned one-shot story and has since evolved into a planned multi-novel series.
